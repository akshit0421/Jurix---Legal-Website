FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is JuriX?+
JuriX is an iOS legal technology app connecting clients with Bar Council verified lawyers. It covers the full journey — finding a lawyer, sending enquiries, encrypted discussions, digital agreement signing, and real-time court updates.
How are lawyers verified on JuriX?+
Lawyers must provide their Bar Council registration ID and a photo of their Bar ID card. JuriX verifies this through an API or manual review before their profile goes live.
How does the enquiry system work?+
Search for a lawyer and tap "Consult" to send an enquiry. The lawyer receives a notification and can accept or decline. You're notified immediately of their response.
What happens after a lawyer accepts my enquiry?+
The case enters In Discussion mode. You and your lawyer can have a secure in-chat. The lawyer uses this phase to understand your case fully before officially taking it on.
How does the agreement signing process work?+
When the lawyer is ready, they upload a legal agreement form through the agreement button. You review, sign, and return it. Once received, the case is officially opened.
Is the chat really private?+
Yes. All messages are end-to-end encrypted — only you and your lawyer can read them. Not even JuriX servers can access the content. No third-party services are involved.
How does the lawyer dashboard work?+
The dashboard shows real-time hearing updates and daily cause lists from official court APIs, highlighting next upcoming and currently ongoing hearings so lawyers are always prepared.
The app is not working. What should I do?+
Force-close and reopen, check your internet, update from the App Store, restart your iPhone. If it persists, email support@jurix.app with your device model and iOS version.
